Let's Explore NITJ

Dr. B R Ambedkar National Institute of Technology Jalandhar (NITJ) is a premier technical institute in India, offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering and technology disciplines. Established in 1987, NITJ is known for its academic excellence, research contributions, and vibrant campus life. The institute boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, modern classrooms, a comprehensive library, and advanced research centers.
