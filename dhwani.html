BHARAT DHWANI


Bharat Dhawani is the techno-managerial student fest of Dr. B. R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology (NIT), Jalandhar in Punjab, India. Bharat Dhawani, the annual fest, has emerged as one of the region’s most sought after event. Just because Bharat Dhawani ’09 was good isn’t the reason enough to bask in your old glory. Bharat Dhawani’11 brings you a whole new level of engineering- “eXtreme engineering”. If you have what it takes to test your limits and put every brain cell to work then come and be a part of Bharat Dhawani’11 .
